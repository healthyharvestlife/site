Fresh and Healthy

We chose (and often help harvest) the freshest most pure ingredients from certified organic farms and prepare them often the same day. Our five-course meals are carefully crafted for you in a facility that does not process any beef, pork, peanuts, shellfish, or products containing gluten. Our quality is delicious.

Fast and Convenient

No time to plan, shop, chop, prep, and cook those healthy tasty meals? No worries. Not only do we deliver right to your door or office, but we take care of all of the work. Other than warming them up, your meals are ready to eat when they arrive. Our customers say we save them at least 45 minutes a day.

Eco-Friendly Feels Good

We are committed to being conscious of our carbon footprint by managing our resources to prevent waste. We only use ingredients that are below 28% on the carbon foot print scale and only harvest what we will use for the next delivery day. We also utilize safe ‘green’ containers that are NSF certified to be commercially sterilized and used again. For every container that we use – we save up to 1,000 plastic or Styrofoam containers from being made and ending up as litter or in our landfills.
